# Source Note: Social Media Today summary of MrBeast thumbnail process

- URL: https://www.socialmediatoday.com/news/youtube-shares-insights-mrbeasts-team-creates-compelling-thumbnail/709817/
- Type: article
- Relevance: summarizes YouTube's interview with MrBeast thumbnail lead Chucky Appleby.

Key evidence:

- MrBeast is known for spending heavily on thumbnail/title creation and generating many thumbnail variants.
- The team develops title and thumbnail before shooting, because they set viewer expectations.
- Appleby emphasizes that thumbnails must deliver trust and align with the video content.
- The image should directly align with the video intro when possible.
- A consistent thumbnail approach helps audiences connect a promise to a known delivery record.
- Appleby says users should understand what is going on the first time they look; slow decoding loses the click.
- The team reportedly develops many title/thumbnail concepts and variations before choosing the final package.

Implication for distillation:

The prompt must start from the click promise and expectation, not from decorative visual traits.
