# Source Note: TIME, Yacht Video Record Analysis

URL: https://time.com/6287028/mrbeast-youtube-views-record/
Source type: article

Relevant takeaways:

- TIME analyzes the yacht video as a strong example of MrBeast's strategy: a giant price contrast, an eye-grabbing thumbnail, a catchy title, and a spectacle that escalates.
- The article points to the billion-dollar price tag as an over-the-top hook.
- It frames the video as a comparison ladder from cheap to absurdly expensive, with the biggest payoff delayed.

Use in distillation:

- For price-ladder videos, show both extremes visually.
- The most expensive or most absurd element should feel impossibly large, pristine, and bright.
- The cheap side should be visibly degraded or low-status.
