# Creator Cover Paradigm Distiller Principles

## Product Form

This product is an Agent-native creator-cover paradigm distiller and router.

It is not a generic cover prompt template, not a PigeonYang-only WeChat cover
tool, and not a standalone UI-first app.

## Core Bet

The durable asset is the growing library of distilled creator-cover engines.

The product advantage is not that AI can generate images. It is that the system
can:

- research a creator's real cover practice,
- turn that practice into a reusable decision engine,
- choose the right engine for a user's article or video,
- translate that engine into original cover rules without copying the creator.

## Layering

- Distillation owns evidence, standards, and child-skill creation.
- Router owns article/video diagnosis and engine selection.
- Production owns private project files, prompt firewall, reference-image
  preflight, output registration, dimension checks, and metrics.

MCP belongs to Production. It is infrastructure, not product intelligence.

## Repository Boundary

- Product repo: reusable skills, standards, specs, scripts, templates, tests.
- `../research-runs`: raw covers, source captures, full research notes, manifests.
- `../cover-projects`: private generated covers, prompts, reviews, metrics.

The repo should keep distilled `design-standard.md` files and generated child
skills, not raw research archives.

## First Application

PigeonYang WeChat article covers are the first application workflow. They should
prove that the system can improve real publishing decisions, but they must not
turn the whole product into a PigeonYang-only cover factory.

## Operating Rules

- Strategy comes before generation.
- Route to a child skill before final prompting.
- If no child skill fits, propose a new distillation target instead of forcing a
  weak engine.
- Copy can be proposed by the Agent, but exact final wording needs approval.
- Final prompts must translate creator patterns into concrete visual rules and
  avoid public creator-name shortcuts.
- Generated outputs are not final until registered and dimension-checked.
